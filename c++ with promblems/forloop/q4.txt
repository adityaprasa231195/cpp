Blast Off!**
*   **Problem statement:** Write a `for` loop that counts backward from 5 to 1. After the loop finishes, print "Blast off!".
*   **Sample input:** None
*   **Sample output:** 
    `5`
    `4`
    `3`
    `2`
    `1`
    `Blast off!`
*   **Hint:** Your initialization should start at 5. Your condition needs to keep running as long as the counter is greater than 0. What should your update statement be to go downwards?
