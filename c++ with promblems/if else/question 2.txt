Problem:
Write a program that takes a person's age and income (per month in rupees) as input. The person is eligible for a government scheme if:

Age is between 18 and 40 (inclusive) AND
Monthly income is less than 15000

Print "Eligible for scheme" or "Not eligible".
Sample Input/Output:
Input: Age = 25, Income = 12000
Output: Eligible for scheme

Input: Age = 35, Income = 20000
Output: Not eligible

Input: Age = 17, Income = 8000
Output: Not eligible
